Eat.Sleep.Develop

My name is David Petersen. I live in rainy Seattle Wasington. I enjoy soccer, traveling, and cooking.

I love traveling, learning, and creating. I was fortunate enough to be able to spend a year in France traveling and working. Adjusting to a new culture was a wonderful experience for me. I fell in love with the food, l'art de vivre, and was fortunate to bring back a girlfriend(now my wife) as well.

I have found that web is a means of creative outlet for me. I started learning HTML and CSS several years ago and have developed a love of trying different web frameworks. I am always on the search for find the means of turning ideas quickly into reality. This is what I have found myself to being truely passionate about, being able to find a problem with the world and try to address it using technology. I don't feel tightly bound to one programming language or another, I want to program in the most terse and simple way as possible.

This website will serve as a outlet for my portfolio and my thoughts on programming.
