Dreaming the October 2012 edition

Its been almost an entire year since my last "dreaming" blog post. I thought it might be a good moment now to revisit some of my ideas from last year as well as write down some new ones.

New Ideas

Notetaking software

This idea has manifested Notepanda a minimalist note taking application that uses Dropbox to persist the notes you take. The idea is still raw, but this is mostly a solved problem with Evernote leading the way.

Lunch Picking Software

I want to build something similar that you can share with co-workers showing a list of places to go and co-workers can drag themselves on to the choices so you can see where people that want to go with lunch for others are going. This could probably be done in an afternoon.

Simple Social Network

See the fewest amount of lines of code required to make a social network API. Then see if it is possible to make a small minimalist frontend application to consume the API.

Old Ideas

I am going to revist some of my ideas from last year. Some of them worked out and some of them didn't work out hardly at all.

Irc Client

This idea manifested itself into Subway the under maintained high potential project under my guidance. Status: In Progress

Addictive language learning

The idea last year was to gamify language learning. At this point I feel like Duolingo does a good job of that. Status: Done

Card encryption

I wanted to make a one off project that would encrypt small messages into a certain card order. Status: Pending

Better playlist creation and sharing

I made a quick page in the playground to look at creating playlists from the music you and your friends post on your facebook wall. Status: Pending

Automation on steroids

Thankfully there are several cool startups taking this on. Both IFTTT and Zapier are both of to a great start. It would be neat to see them increase the amount of complexity so you could funnel data and actions through a few filters. Status: Done
